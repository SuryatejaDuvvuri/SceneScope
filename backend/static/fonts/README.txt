Place DejaVuSans.ttf and DejaVuSans-Bold.ttf here for Unicode PDF export support.
You can download them from:
- https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/
- Or directly: https://github.com/dejavu-fonts/dejavu-fonts/blob/master/ttf/DejaVuSans.ttf
- And: https://github.com/dejavu-fonts/dejavu-fonts/blob/master/ttf/DejaVuSans-Bold.ttf

After placing the files, the export will support Unicode characters.
