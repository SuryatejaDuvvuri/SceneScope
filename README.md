## SceneScope: A writing tool that shows you what your words actually say

### Problem
Writers and directors often face a gap between what is written and how it is interpreted visually. This disconnect can lead to miscommunication, wasted resources, and creative frustration. Bridging this gap is essential for effective storytelling and production.

### Solution
SceneScope creates a feedback loop for creators: write → see → refine → repeat. By instantly visualizing written scenes, users can better understand how their words translate to the screen, enabling rapid iteration and clearer communication.
